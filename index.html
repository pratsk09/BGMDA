Learn about BGMDA

BGMDA stands for Buffalo Grove Muscular Dystrophy Awareness. Our organization focuses on helping more people understand muscular dystrophy and how it can affect students, families, and communities.

What is muscular dystrophy?

Muscular dystrophy refers to a group of conditions that cause muscles to become weaker over time. It can affect movement, independence, and daily life.

Why awareness matters

Awareness helps students and community members become more understanding, supportive, and informed about the challenges people affected by muscular dystrophy may face.

How BGMDA helps

BGMDA works to spread information, support students, organize outreach, and encourage community involvement.
